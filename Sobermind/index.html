sober mind services - liberia (sms-liberia)

This is a non-profit organization established to assist teenagers, youths and young adults develop and maintain a normal way of thinking and behaving. This organization was giving birth on November 10, 2021 by Rev. Brandy D. Wolova, a Liberian Humanitarian and trauma facilitator.

Background

sober mind services-liberia
(SMS-liberia)

SMS-Liberia's programs are designed to primarily alleviate and minimize the global outcry of parents, guardians and government over the destruction of the souls or minds and destinies of their children and wayward citizens who are been affected daily by negative behaviors and hobbies: lack of self-esteem, the use of drugs or substance abuse and other social vices.

Vision
Engaging teenagers, youths and young adults to think, act and live soberly.

mission Statement

To facilitate the process by which young people can build their destinies; assist victimized youths become rehabilitated and reintegrated.

To guide teenagers, youths and young people to be able to resist and avoid the mediums and activities that will lure their souls into risk and non-productive behaviour and lifestyles

Core Values

Loving, Sharing and Caring

Prohibiting discrimination and domination, yet treat all teenagers, youth and young adults with dignity and equall respect(nontheless, give priority and preffernce to females).

Promoting youth education.
that is academic, religious, and vocational(Skill Training); and ensure that every youth has equal opportunity to develop his/her full potential.

What we do at SMS - Liberia Inc.?

Develop the minds of teenagers, youths and young adults.

Prioritize teenagers, youths and young adults and assist them develop self-confidence, esteem and dignity.

Reach out to vulnerable teenagers, youths and young adults languishing in ghettos, street corners and else where, and provide temporary shelter for the homeless ones during the period of counseling and rehablitation

Devlopeing the intellect of members & clients.

Enabling psychosocial support to members and clients

Enabling physical development and Providing economic initiatives for self-empowerment
